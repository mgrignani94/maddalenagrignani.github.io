Maddalena Grignani

PhD Student in Economics

Pompeu Fabra University, Barcelona

I'm interested in understanding how public policy can address social problems. My research focuses on the effects of sex education on gender gaps in labor force participation and intimate partner violence (IPV), as well as the relationship between access to healthcare and fertility. I have also worked on institutional trust.

Advisor: Libertad González.
